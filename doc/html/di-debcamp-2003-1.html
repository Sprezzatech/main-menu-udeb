Debcamp for debian-installer

To speed up the development of debian-installer, the foundation behind Skolelinux believe it is useful to sponsor another debcamp for the d-i developers. The debcamp should be low-cost, and located somewhat close to most developers to keep transport costs low. The initial idea is to place it somewhere in Germany, as several developers are located in Europe. Kurt Gramlich has volunteered to look at the local organizing part.

Organizers

Debian-side: Petter Reinholdtsen

Local side: Kurt Gramlich

Date and location

The date and location is not yet decied, but two locations are currently being looked at.

Bildungsstätte Einschlingen have room for 15 people from Monday 203-10-27 to Friday 2003-10-31. There is DSL internet available there.

Oldenburger LinuxTag: Entwicklertreffen, south of Bremen/Hamburg, might be a nice place to have it. They plan a gathering 2003-09-25 to 2003-09-28.
