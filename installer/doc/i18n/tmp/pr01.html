This document must be maintained up-to-date according to processes and methods by the D-I internationalisation (i18n) coordinator.

D-I packages maintainers who only need to know what they have to do in D-I packages for following D-I i18n rules should rather read the "i18n/maintainers.txt" document.

D-I team members who want to understand how translations are handled in D-I and related packages should rather read the "i18n/technical.txt" document.

This document also explains how to get l10n for Debian packages involved during the installation process though not strictly speaking part of Debian Installer.
